Description

an combine multiple microbes in a single product BEE-NPK is a combination of Nitrogen-Fixing, Phosphate Solubilizing, and Potash Mobilizing Bacteria in Lyophilized form which helps for prolonged crop growth and higher yield. Enter plant roots and increase the amount of nitrogen compound in the crop. It solubilizes available potash and phosphorous in the soil Synthesizes auxin, vitamins, nicotinic acid, gibberellins, which help the plant in better germination, early emergence, and better root development. Promote disease resistance and drought tolerance. Stimulates growth and gives greens color characteristics. Encourages plumpness and succulence of fruits & increases protein percentage. It’s a carrier-based consortia biofertilizer containing plant probiotic microbes.
